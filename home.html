Nice to meet you 😎
This is Minjung, studying UX and CMC

I am a Master's student in the Color Lab, KAIST, under the advisement of Prof. Hyeon-Jeong Suk.
Through the research, I aim to understand users' emotional change and communication in variety of virtual contexts.

My research interest focuses on the AI-MC(AI-mediated communication), CMC(Computer-mediated communication), User experience, and Social Media.
I am also broadly interested in the following keywords - conversational user interface (CUI), AMC(Avatar-mediated communication), emotion and Facial expression in virtual environment.

I also have professional experience as UX designer @CJ Livecity and other startup companies.
